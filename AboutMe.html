About Me

Hello, my name is Nikole Ralston and I am a student at the University of Advancing Technology.
I have anxiety and know what it feels like to procrastinate until the last minute.
So I made this website to help those like me get on top of their homework.
This site will let users put in their work and you are reminded when their work is due up to 5 days in advance.
There are also people there to help and guide you through it, so you are not panicking.

Why The Desgin?

I chose the loft girl design because she is very calming and helps people get through their work with the soft beats that play in the background.
She reminds me that no matter what I got to get my work done.
